Avocart

The freshest way to organize and track your grocery shopping trips.

✨ Features

  • Smart Checklists: Organize items by category with a beautiful, intuitive UI.
  • Spending Statistics: Visualize your monthly grocery budget with elegant bar charts.
  • Receipt Vault: Attach multiple receipt photos to your trips to keep track of every cent.
  • Custom Categories: Create personalized categories like "Camping" or "Party" for better organization.
  • iCloud Backup & Restore: Keep your shopping history and receipts safe and synced.

📱 Availability

Avocart is available for iPhone and iPad via the Apple App Store.
