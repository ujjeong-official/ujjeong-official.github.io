Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026 March 10th

Avocart (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

  • Shopping Data: All grocery lists, items, and price entries are stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud. We do not transmit this data to our own servers.
  • Receipt Photos: Photos of your receipts are stored on your device's local storage and synced via your private iCloud account.
  • Purchases: Payment processing is handled by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive your credit card details.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide shopping organization, calculate spending stats, and store receipt images locally.
  • To back up and sync your data across your devices via iCloud (if enabled).

3. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store (in-app purchases)
  • iCloud (data synchronization managed by Apple)

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We do not store your data on external servers. You can delete your data at any time by deleting lists within the app or removing the app from your device.
