About the Game

Grabity is a minimalist yet challenging arcade game built with SwiftUI and SpriteKit. Test your reflexes, survive against gravity, and climb the leaderboard.

Features

  • Fast, smooth gameplay with intuitive controls
  • Dynamic obstacles and increasing difficulty
  • Minimal design with engaging effects
  • Local best score tracking
  • Optional ad-free experience via in-app purchase
