Support

Effective Date: September 12, 2025

Thank you for playing Grabity! Below you will find support information and ways to reach out if you need help.

1. Common Questions

  • Ads not showing or too frequent? Ads are provided by third-party networks. Sometimes availability varies. You can remove ads permanently via in-app purchase.
  • Restore Purchases? If you reinstalled the App or changed devices, tap the “Restore Purchases” button in settings to unlock your ad-free purchase again.
  • Game progress lost? Grabity stores progress locally. Deleting the App also deletes local progress. At this time, cloud sync is not available.
