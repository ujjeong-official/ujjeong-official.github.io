Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 12, 2025

Grabity (“the App”) is a casual iOS game. We respect your privacy and keep data collection minimal. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

  • No Personal Data: The App does not require an account and does not collect personally identifiable information.
  • Local Data: Gameplay state (e.g., best score, theme, ad-removal status) is stored locally on your device using Apple frameworks.
  • Purchases: Payments (e.g., Remove Ads) are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not access your credit card or billing details.
  • Advertising: Ads are shown via third-party networks (e.g., Google AdMob). These may collect device information such as ad identifier, approximate location (IP-based), and performance metrics.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide gameplay features and save progress locally.
  • To process in-app purchases via Apple.
  • To display and measure ads through third-party networks.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit (in-app purchases)
  • Apple frameworks (local storage, system services)
  • Advertising SDKs (e.g., Google AdMob)

4. Data Retention & Deletion

All gameplay data remains on your device unless you delete the App. Purchases and advertising data are retained by Apple and ad partners under their own policies.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Your Choices

  • You can manage ad tracking and privacy settings under iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • You can reset the device Advertising Identifier in iOS settings.
  • You may restore purchases via the App’s “Restore Purchases” button.
